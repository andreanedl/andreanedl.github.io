Structure

The structure is made of T-Slots and V-Slots. A V-Slot is basically a T-Slot with a 45 degrees cut so the wheels can slide on it. V-Slots are more expensive so I use them only on the moving axes. They are easy to cut since they are made of aluminum and no need to drill any holes, just use T-Nuts where you need a screw.
