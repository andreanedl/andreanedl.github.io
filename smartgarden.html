Control


Watering

The first attempt was to use an air pump creating pressure in a plastic bottle which ejects the water out of it. I used an extra pump I had from a previous project. For ShotBot, it's very important to use an air pump and not a suction pump to keep the soda carbonated. In this case, it was creating a syphon since the exit is lower than the water level in the bottle. For Smart Garden, the pump would turn off, but the water would keep flowing until the water level is underneath the water exit. I finally replaced the air pump by a suction pump and it works perfectly now.
